How does BizNet work?


Match Contacts

With a smart matching algorithm BizNet finds the right contact to invite and start networking with. You can also discover and search manually to connect and invite your existing network to BizNet. To interact with your new contact BizNet suggest CTA's as next best action like message, video call, phone call or a lunch meeting.


Find business

All users automatically receive gigs which are matching their profile and skillset. This is a social marketplace feature. You apply through the app and get contacted by the gig owner. To build trust and reputation you can get rated or recommended by the gig owner or a user who you have helped. Users can post gigs within their network or publicly (pro version).


Stay updated

The feed is the core engagement feature to keep you updated about what is happening in your network. BizNet notifies you to various events that you should act on to create more value and rewards when interacting with your network.
